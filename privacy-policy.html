Privacy Policy

At Wicket Pickz, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform. By accessing or using Wicket Pickz, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from our users:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name: Used for account creation and personalization.
  • Email Address: Required for communication, account verification, and transactional updates.
  • Payment Information: Collected securely via third-party payment processors for transactions (e.g., entry fees, withdrawals).

1.2 Non-Personal Information

  • Device Information: Includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Tracks how you interact with our platform, such as pages visited, features used, and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to enhance functionality and user experience.

1.3 Sensitive Information

  • Location Data: If required, to ensure compliance with legal regulations regarding contest eligibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage user accounts.
  • Service Delivery: To provide you access to contests, leaderboards, and other platform features.
  • Payment Processing: To facilitate secure transactions and withdrawals.
  • Personalization: To offer tailored content and recommendations based on your preferences.
  • Communication: send updates, promotions, and important notifications.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal requirements, including age and location verification.
  • Analytics: To analyze user behavior and improve our platform’s performance and usability.

3. Sharing of Information

We value your privacy and do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your information in the following scenarios:

  • With Service Providers: Trusted third-party partners who assist in payment processing, analytics, and customer support.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales involving Wicket Pickz.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on Wicket Pickz. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the functionality of the platform.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform.
  • Advertising Cookies: Deliver personalized ads and promotions.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect certain functionalities of the platform.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. These include:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted during transmission.
  • Secure Servers: Hosted in secure environments with restricted access.
  • Regular Audits: Periodic reviews of our security practices.

6. Your Rights

As a user of Wicket Pickz, you have the following rights:

  • Access and Correction: Request access to or correction of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Obtain a copy of your data in a structured format.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.
  • Account Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at info@wicketPickz.com.
