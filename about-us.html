About

Welcome to Wicket Pickz, where your love for cricket meets the excitement of strategy, competition, and rewards. We are more than just a fantasy cricket platform—we are a hub for cricket enthusiasts who want to immerse themselves in the game like never before. Whether you are a casual fan or a die-hard cricket strategist, Wicket Pickz is designed to give you an unparalleled experience.

Our Story

Wicket Pickz was born from a shared passion for cricket and technology. Our founders, avid cricket fans themselves, saw an opportunity to combine the thrill of the sport with the power of fantasy gaming to create something extraordinary.

Our journey began with one simple goal: to enhance the way fans interact with the game. Cricket is more than a sport; it's a global phenomenon that unites people across cultures and nations. Wicket Pickz is our way of celebrating that spirit by allowing fans to become a part of the action, strategize like a captain, and compete like a pro—all from the comfort of their devices.

From humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a robust platform powered by cutting-edge technology, driven by user feedback, and sustained by our unwavering love for the game.

Our Mission

Our mission is to redefine the cricket-watching experience by offering a fantasy platform that is:

  • Engaging: Making every match more thrilling by letting fans participate actively.
  • Accessible: A user-friendly interface that caters to both beginners and experienced fantasy players.
  • Rewarding: Offering exciting prizes and rewards that enhance the joy of the game.

At Wicket Pickz, we are committed to fostering a community where cricket fans can connect, compete, and celebrate their passion for the sport.
