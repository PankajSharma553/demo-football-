Personal Data Policy

At Wicket Pickz, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Personal Data Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services. By using our platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy. Please take the time to read through the policy carefully.

1. Data We Collect

We collect personal data in various ways, including when you register an account, interact with our platform, and communicate with us. The types of personal data we collect include:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, date of birth, and contact details.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, payment methods, and transaction history.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device information.
  • Communication Data: Messages you send to us, including feedback, support requests, and any other communications.
  • Location Data: geographical location based on your IP address or provided information, where applicable.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Account Creation and Management: To provide you with access to our platform and manage your user account.
  • Service Improvement: To understand how our platform is used and improve its functionality and user experience.
  • Communication: To send you relevant information regarding contests, promotions, updates, and support inquiries.
  • Payment Processing: To process payments and manage any transactions related to your use of our services.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, such as fraud prevention and dispute resolution.

3. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, analytics providers, etc.) who assist us in operating our platform and delivering services to you.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your data if required to do so by law, regulation, or legal process, or if we believe it is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Wicket Pickz, our users, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring party.

All third parties we share your data with are obligated to handle it securely and in accordance with this policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. This includes keeping your data for legal, accounting, or reporting purposes. If you delete your account, we may retain certain information for a reasonable period to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

5. Security of Your Data

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include encryption, firewalls, secure payment processing, and restricted access to data.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances, such as when the data is no longer necessary for the purposes it was collected.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: If we process your data based on consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time, without affecting the legality of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
  • Data Portability: You can request to receive your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and have it transmitted to another controller, if technically feasible.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@wicketpickz.com.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. Cookies are small files stored on your device that allow us to remember your preferences and improve the functionality of the site. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

We may also use third-party analytics tools to help us understand how our platform is used. These tools may collect information such as your IP address, device type, and browser type.

8. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the country where our servers are based, your personal data may be transferred to and processed in a different jurisdiction. By using our platform, you consent to such transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Personal Data Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date of the updated policy will be reflected at the top of the page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
